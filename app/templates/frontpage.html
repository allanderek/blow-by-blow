Blow-by-Blow

Welcome to blow-by-blow. Here we assist your live-text commentary on any and all events. We like live-text commentary, traditional news outlets have something of a monopoly on live-text commentary. Others can take to twitter, but tend to annoy followers who aren't interested in that particular event. In addition, perhaps twitter does not always cater especially well for live-text commentary. So the idea here is that you start a feed, and send any potential viewers to your live-text feed.

You might live-text about a sporting event, a political event, a current news event. You might do so simply as an interested party or you might do so as someone with particular access to the event. This might simply be living close to a currently unfolding major news event, or it might be that you have back-stage access to an entertainment event. Whatever it is, if you feel like the world might like updates from you about an event, start a feed and start giving your observations, comments, opinions, and headlines.

There is no need to create an account to start one. In fact we do not even yet have any notion of an account. Start a feed and you will be directed to the special author page, provided you do not navigate away from that page and lose the URL to it, you'll still be able to update your feed. Viewers of your feed will only be able to view it.

If you would like you can start a feed.
